Sitaramji Ganorkar Junior College

Classes XI and XII are introduced in the year 2013. All students after obtaining a clear Pass Certificate in the class X Board Examination i.e. ICSE, SSC, CBSE are welcome to join the Junior College. The Junior College offers Science stream. At this level of the school that students have to decide what career they wish in pursue. Our experienced staff guides them in the right direction and let them through this journey of two critical years with dedication and responsibility, ensuring that they are admitted to the universities of their choice after passing out.

Scheme of Examination :

The scheme of Examination is defined by Maharashtra State Board is as follows:

1. Class XI examination will be conducted by the college.

2. 80% attendance compulsory for promotion to the next class.

3. Class XII examination will be conducted by Maharashtra State Board.


Admission Procedure

1. Every student admitted in the college will have to pay fees as per the college norms.

2. Amount of fees shall not be refunded if the student leaves the college for any reason after the admission.

3. Parents / Guardians of the candidate will be required to submit.


Important:

1. Admission form

2. Original Transfer Certificate(T.C)

3. Original marklist of last session.

4. Original caste & Income certificate

5. Original migration certificate (for the students coming from other college)

6. Three recent passport sized photograph of the students

7. Prescribed fees

8. Aadhar card of students & parents
